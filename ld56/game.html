How to play

Click and drag to order your worker ants to (by default) dig and build dirt.

Use the right mouse button or the Cancel mode to cancel orders.

Use arrow keys, or drag the middle mouse button to pan around the map, and use the scroll wheel to zoom.

Dirt with no orders to build will be brought to the nearest air space and become a wall for ants to climb on and solid dirt to be built on.

Find food to bring to the Queen to produce grubs that will grow into new worker ants.

Dig tunnels and build structures to divert water to keep your colony safe from drowning. Rainfall will come and go, but gradually get heavier over time!

Use the appropriate order modes to designate grub nurseries for ants to bring new grubs to, and set the Queen home to have the Queen be moved.
